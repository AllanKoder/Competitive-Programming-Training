## Mistakes

- Not setting a time limit with questions
- A singular scrap paper
- Not taking breathings exercises


## Learnings

- Keep it simplier in the earlier rounds
- Reread the questions to ensure that you have things clearly understood
- Write down meta cognition ideas
- Take breaths to relax the mind
- Don't jump to coding unless you have the entire solution in your head
